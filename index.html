Groslen is a data analytics startup



A little bit about our app

Our app will allow user to see the line of a bar in real time and how close the bar is to max capacity using various data sources. This application will allow user feel like they are more aware of what the nightlife is like where they are going out, whether that be where they live, college campus or if you're traveling aboard. User's will be able to sign up with their Facebook accounts, doing this will allow Groslen users to see their friends in a closed platform. 

About our team

We're a group of Computer Science majors attending Marist College in Poughkeepsie, NY. We came up with the idea to build our bar analytics application once the weather started getting colder and standing in long bar lines became unbearable. We had to find a solution for this, which is why we set out to make an app that will tell you how long the line is at a bar, how close it is to max capacity and who was at what bar without broadcasting it to the world.
